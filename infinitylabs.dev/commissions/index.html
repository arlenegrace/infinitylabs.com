Frequently Asked Questions

How is the pricing determined?

Hourly rates heavily depend on what your website requires. A static portfolio website written entirely in HTML and CSS will cost less than a YouTube clone that requires extensive use of programming languages like JavaScript. Please contact us for a quote and a full breakdown of costs.

Why not website builders?

While website builders are very cost effective, they are often limited in features and most will not allow you to migrate away from their platform. The website you make using a website builder belongs to that company, not you. If you require anything not available on a particular platform, you must rebuild the entire website. This ends up being more expensive in the long term.

Is a custom website a good fit for my company?

For young startups and small businesses, we recommend opting for our template service as it is the most cost effective option with minimal compromises. If you have established your product-market fit, you have a large number of webpages, you need lots of flexibility and personalization, or an extensive use of programming languages, we recommend a bespoke website as they are able to scale with your business.

Why custom?

With a custom website, the goal is to grow your business and strengthen your brand. The layout and order in which things appear on your website matters, and the links in your navigation bar provide a quick overview of what your website is about. A custom website will ensure your users get where they need to be with minimal obstructions.
