Bespoke Commission

Custom designed website created specifically for you

We work directly with you by first getting to know you, your brand and its needs. Research will then begin on how to best market your services and convert page visits to sales and new clients/customers. This will include statistics from studies, surveys and other sources. UI and UX design will then begin and a mockup render will be created. Once the design is finalized, any changes will be billed under a new invoice to maintain schedule and deadlines.

Process

  1. Client requests quote with description of project.
  2. Client pays cost of designing.
  3. Mockup of website will be designed to the client's liking.
  4. Cost of website will be calculated and client will pay up-front.
  5. Project to be made and sent to client.

Build Service

Your designs, we build it

You provide us with the image mockups illustrating exactly how the website will look, and we will build it to your spec. Please include mockups for multiple screen sizes including desktop, tablet and a smartphone. Media such as images and logos will be supplied by the client.

Process

  1. Client requests quote with description of project and image renders.
  2. Cost of website will be calculated and client will pay up-front.
  3. Website to be made and sent to client.

Template Service

Get our templates up in under a day

Starting from $100 USD, the template setup service is a great option for small businesses with tight budgets and minimal compromises. Pick any of our free templates, provide the images and text to use, and we will send over a completed website. You may also request to have additional sections added to a template, or have small alterations made to fit your needs.

Process

  1. Client requests quote with the template they would like.
  2. Cost of service will be calculated and client will pay up-front.
  3. Website to be completed and sent to client.
