ENTHUSIAST

Technology has become an important part of our lives with much of our time spent interacting with it. For something so integral, why compromise on something less?

Infinity Labs

Fascinated by technology from a young age, Mark set out to explore the bleeding edge of consumer electronics. He later created Infinity Labs to share the experience with others interested in tech products. Appreciating the engineering required to create multi-display laptops and laptops with desktop components, and discovering new use cases that may further advance and bring those technologies to the mainstream.

The Team

Our story began with a group of friends looking to create a startup. As the member responsible for front end design and development, rapid progress was made in order to maintain deadlines. Designs were always made with usability in mind — ensuring the website would always behave the ways users expect, and the code was written from the perspective of a software developer — code deduplication, maintainability and readability. Having discovered a passion for web design and development, infinitylabs.dev was created with the ultimate goal of modernizing websites, with other members from the startup joining to offer additional services including web hosting and software development with JavaScript, C# and Rust.

Our Mission

Some designers only design for common resolutions, or create designs that simply cannot be implemented due to the limitations of current languages. Some begin to fall apart with higher resolution displays, others have poor element placement such as positioning the menu button on the top left for smartphones — introducing reachability issues with one-handed use on larger devices for right-handed users.

Good design requires a fine balance between usability, information, and aesthetics. It must be simple to navigate between pages, bodies of text must be informative without being too long so as to bore the user, and an aesthetically pleasing design gives the user a sense of confidence in your brand. We endeavour to design and create websites without compromising any of these three fundamentals.
